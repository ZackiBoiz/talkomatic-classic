Multiplayer Piano sound samples

These piano note samples are from the Salamander Grand Piano (Yamaha C5),
recorded by Alexander Holm, and are licensed under Creative Commons
Attribution 3.0 (CC-BY 3.0): https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

The reduced web set (one velocity layer, sampled every minor third and
pitch-shifted in the browser to cover all 88 keys) is the same set distributed
with Tone.js: https://github.com/Tonejs/Tone.js

Files in this folder: A0, C1, Ds1, Fs1, A1, ... C8 (.mp3), one per sampled note.
